OUR HISTORY

A Legacy of Excellence in Leather Care

Furniture Laundry, founded in 2017, specializes in cleaning, restoring, dyeing, and repairing high-end leather furniture. With a focus on understanding the nuances of leather, we have perfected techniques and adopted state-of-the-art technologies to deliver unparalleled results. With decades of experience, we are the go-to choice for homeowners, businesses, and designers, ensuring their furniture remains stunning and in good condition. We offer worry-free maintenance and timeless elegance.
